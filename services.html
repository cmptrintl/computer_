Our Services

We charge only for the work we actually deliver — planning, scoping, and research are always free.

Pricing is tailored to your needs and provided upon request, once we understand your scope and whether you are a private or business client.

IT & Technical Support

From basic troubleshooting to advanced infrastructure projects — we provide reliable support when you need it, remotely or on-site.

  • General computer help, email setup, and networking
  • System setup, hosting, DNS configuration, mail servers
  • Security hardening, firewalls, virtualization, mobile device management
  • Audits, backup planning, and ongoing maintenance

End-to-End Company Setup

Perfect for new business owners — we handle everything from legal setup to your first client presentation.

  • Name Request & Incorporation (BC or Federal)
  • Operating Name (DBA) registration
  • Business Licence applications
  • CRA/BN, GST/PST registration
  • Domains, email accounts, and website hosting
  • Records Office setup and corporate minute book

Corporate Identity & Branding

We create a cohesive, professional identity for your brand — consistent across all media.

  • Naming & slogan development
  • Logo, typography, and colour palette
  • Brand guidelines and style sheets
  • UX/UI design for web and apps
  • Photography, stock curation, or 3D renderings

Digital Solutions

  • Website design, hosting, and CDN integration
  • Email deliverability optimization (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
  • Local network and VLAN design
  • System monitoring and secure remote access
  • Security policy creation, implementation, and staff onboarding to ensure consistent protection across all systems

Specialized Services

  • 3D scanning & reverse engineering
  • Hardware modding and professional AV systems
  • Drone photography, Matterport, and photorealistic rendering

Event & Presentation Technology

Professional setups for events, presentations, and entertainment — fully managed on-site. From immersive visuals to interactive experiences, we bring your event to life.

  • Outdoor cinema with 4K projection, large screen, and surround sound
  • Karaoke and live audio for parties, weddings, and corporate events
  • Photo Booth Services — fun, high-quality instant prints or digital sharing for weddings, parties, and corporate functions
